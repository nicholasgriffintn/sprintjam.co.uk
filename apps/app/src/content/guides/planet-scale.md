## Why use the planet scale?

Planet scale is playful, which makes it useful for energising a room. The names carry a sense of scale without pulling you into numbers.

It is best for early discussions where you want relative size and a bit of momentum.

## Card sequence

Moon, Earth, Mars, Jupiter, Saturn, Uranus, Neptune, Pluto.

## When it works best

- Kick-off workshops and discovery sessions
- Teams that benefit from a lighter tone
- Rough ordering of mixed-size work

## How to run a round

1. Agree on what an Earth feels like.
2. Vote privately and reveal together.
3. Capture any big differences in assumptions.

## Watch-outs

- It is intentionally subjective; avoid using it for velocity tracking.
- If you need precise capacity, switch to a numeric scale.
